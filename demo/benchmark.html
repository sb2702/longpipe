Real-world FPS: Longpipe vs MediaPipe

Streams /loop_video.mp4 through a WebCodecs pipeline (demux → decode → segment + composite → encode), once per backend. Total elapsed wall clock ÷ frames processed = real FPS, not bounded by display refresh. Both backends produce VideoFrames at the source video's resolution with an image virtual background. First few frames are dropped from timing as warmup.
