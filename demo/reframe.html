Auto-reframe v0 — scratch pad

Throwaway. The logic runs on the CPU here so it can be iterated on with sliders; production will keep the state in a carrier tensor and apply the crop as a sampling transform in the compositor (no readback, both backends). This page is for finding numbers that feel right — porting them is mechanical.

The rule. The frame centre is the base; the subject applies gravity pulling the crop centre toward it — at gravity < 1 the subject never gets centred, it just gets closer. The crop keeps the frame's aspect and shrinks by zoom. Two hard constraints: it must stay inside the frame and must contain the subject with margin. A head in the extreme corner therefore does nothing at all, with no special case — containing it would need a crop ≈ the whole frame, i.e. zoom ≈ 1. Status reads NO-OP when that happens.

auto re-solves every frame and moves with a deadband — hold still, move deliberately, hold again. manual solves once when you hit Reframe now and then freezes (the Meet-style button). body blends the alpha mask's centroid into the target; at 0 it's face-only and skips the mask readback entirely.
